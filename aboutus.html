About Us

Dr.RaghaviRavichandran MBBS,FCD,FAM the founder of Touch Aesthetics is a dedicated and experienced aesthetic physician with a passion for enhancing natural beauty and helping individuals feel confident in their own skin. With a background in Fellowship in Aesthetic Medicine COCTRASI, Dr.Raghavi Ravichandran combines advanced medical knowledge with an artistic eye, ensuring that every treatment is tailored to the unique needs of each patient. After completing extensive training in the latest aesthetic procedures, Dr. Raghavi Ravichandran established Touch Aesthetics to offer a personalized approach to non-surgical cosmetic treatments. Driven by a commitment to safety, results, and patient satisfaction, Dr. Raghavi Ravichandran continuously pursues the latest advancements in aesthetic medicine, ensuring that patients receive the highest standard of care. With a warm, patient-centered philosophy, Dr. Raghavi Ravichandran creates an environment where clients feel comfortable and confident.
